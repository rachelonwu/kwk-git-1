My Coding Journey

2021

I began my web development journey with a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, making a personal protfolio website (linked below), a personality quiz, and an activist toolkit website.

2022

I participated in a few hackathons and expanded on my HTML and CSS skills.

2023

Through dual enrollment, I took a college-level web development course. I also took AP Computer Science A and Principles, learning Java. During the summer, I participated in the Kode With Klossy Mobile App Development Camp, using Swift and SwiftUI through XCode. I co-developed an app focused on limiting food waste by providing simple recipes with ingredients commonly found in households. Later that summer, I worked as a Software Development Intern at rrtQ Inc, learning the basics of SQL and Figma.

2024

I took another dual enrollment class for beginner's Python. I also ran my school's Computer Science Club and promoted/attended a few hackathons throughout the year focused on web development.

2025

In my first year of college, I took a beginner and intermediate Python course. In the spring, I began a research internship at Massachusetts General Hospital, using R to analyzing user data of an app focused on providing mentalizing imagery therapy to caregivers of dementia patients. I am currently in this role, and am also working as an IT intern for Skyla Credit Union, using my web development skills to fix HTML pages and design a program to automate account number changes.
